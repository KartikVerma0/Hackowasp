HAND ME DOWN/
CLOTH DONATION


DONATE NOT DUMP YOUR OLD CLOTHES

About The Event

This is a drive campaign where we encourage people to donate their old clothes for someone else to use. Also This is not just limited to clothes rather old blankets, quilts, bags etc are also very much welcome and encouraged.



GOAL OF EVENT

Our aim is to try and ensure that your old clothes do not end up in the dump and rather are given to someone who strive very had to make their day to day ends meet.



HOW TO DONATE

Reach out to the contact given and drop off your used clothes for someone else to use. Also u can send the clothes via mail to the address mentioned.
